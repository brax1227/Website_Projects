Welcome To ROME LLC

Welcome to ROME LLC, where innovation meets engineering excellence. Founded by a group of 5 engineers who crossed paths during the Business Engineering Technology minor at Auburn University, ROME is driven by a shared passion for solving realworld challenges. Alex, having grown up on a poultry farm, recognized the industry's need for innovation. This insight inspired us to develop the Cow-Paw device, marking it as the inaugural product from ROME. We have a mission to innovate and create solutions that simplify the lives of farmers and workers. At ROME, we are not just engineers; we are innovators with a deep understanding of the impact engineering can have on people's lives. We believe in the power of engineering minds to solve problems, improve efficiency, and pave the way for a better tomorrow.
